Two days will forever be the worst days of my life. June 14th - my son drowned while at swim class. He was found unresponsive in the deep end of the pool. June 15th is when his heart stopped and didn’t start back. I remember the morning of June 14th, taking my healthy and happy 4-year-old to his swim class not knowing what was about to happen. I remember the feelings and thoughts after walking to the pool and finding him laying on the poolside unresponsive. Immediately, I wished I could have traded places with him. He didn’t deserve the neglect given by his swim instructor (no child does). I remember thinking what his final thoughts would have been: in his mind calling out to me or thinking any moment now I'll be saved by Mom.
